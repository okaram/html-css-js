We are affiliated with the Christian Church (Disciples of Christ), a major Protestant denomination with 800,000 members nationwide. FIrst Christian Church (Disciplesof Chirst) of Marietta was founded in 1949.

For more information, please visit the General Church website and the Regional Church wesite.

We proclaim Jesus as Savior and Lord, and draw our inspiration from the Bible. Our particular mission is for the unity of the church. We celebrate weekly around rthe Lord's Table, the life, death, resurrection and continuing presence of our Lord.

We are a diverse mix of individuals and families from a number of backgrounds. Our friendly family sized, congregation provides a family atmosphere where everyone can get to know and support one another.

Our worship is traditional with a blend of contemporary features, including drama and music. Communion for all is celebrated weekly. A Chancel Choir and congregational singing highlight our worship services. We also feature a contemporary praise music band known as Soufire. Our ministers base his/her sermons on Scripture, but relate the themes of the Bible to contemporary living. If you want to follow along, Bibles are provided in the sanctuary.

Please dress the way that you feel comfortable! We're more concerned with meeting your real life needs than with what you wear.
